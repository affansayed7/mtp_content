Viva questions
Q: What is IMSI?
Unique 15-digit identity of a mobile subscriber — MCC + MNC + MSIN.
Q: What does a SIM store?
IMSI (identity) and Ki (secret authentication key).
Q: Why is authentication needed?
Because IMSI alone can be copied — authentication with Ki proves the subscriber is genuine.
Q: Does registration mean internet access?
No. Internet requires PDU Session Establishment after registration.
⭐ A SIM contains subscriber identity (IMSI) and secret credentials (Ki). The network authenticates using Ki, completes registration, and only then establishes a PDU session to provide internet access.
