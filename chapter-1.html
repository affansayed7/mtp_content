Viva question
Q: What is the first thing a cellular network must do when a phone powers on?
Authenticate and register the subscriber — verifying identity, validity, and authorisation before any internet access is granted.
⭐ A cellular network exists to provide authenticated, mobile, large-scale connectivity — maintaining user identity, mobility, and internet access simultaneously.
